Margaritas and Mexican Food Date Night

We recently went on two fun margarita and Mexican food excursions, one highbrow and one lowbrow. Both were fun and both featured bars, restraunts or food trucks we would reccommend. Fancy, or fun with a dash of DIY, both were a good time.

The first was a highbrow date night that started at Maxine's Taproom for pre-dinner cocktails and moved to the Tula, the recently opend high end Mexican restraunt, for dinner and dessert.

The more affordable option was a fun Saturday afternoon picnic featuring a thermos of DIY Magragitas and a bike ride to the food truck court near Lake Fayetteville for tacos and fruit ices.
