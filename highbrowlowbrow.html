Margaritas and Mexican Food Date Night

We recently went on two fun margarita and Mexican food excursions, one highbrow and one lowbrow. Both were fun and both featured bars, restraunts or food trucks we would reccommend.

Fancy, or fun with a dash of DIY, both were a good time.

Craft Cocktails and Fine Dining Vs. DIY Drinks and a Taco Truck Picnic

Happy hour at Maxine's is a chill experience but it gets louder and more crowded as the night goes on. Bottom line: I make a good cocktail but they make a better one. Dinner at Tula Tacos at Lake Fayetteville Food Trucks
Price $50 Price $15
Rating ***** Rating *****
Tula is a new reeturant on the Fayetteville square specializing in higher end Mexican cuisine. Its menu features items not found on your usual Tex Mex restraunt menu. It has a lovley atmosphere and is suitable for a date night without being too stuffy. Bottom line: the food and atmosphere was worth the price if you are looking for an upscale date night spot.

Bottomline:Interesting menu, quality food, nice atmosphere. do it if you want upscale.
